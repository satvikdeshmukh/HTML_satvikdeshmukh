Data Science

Data science is an interdisciplinary field that involves the use of scientific methods, algorithms, processes, and systems to extract knowledge and insights from structured and unstructured data. It combines elements of mathematics, statistics, computer science, domain expertise, and communication skills to understand complex phenomena, make predictions, and drive decision-making.

In essence, data science encompasses the entire process of gathering, cleaning, analyzing, visualizing, and interpreting data to uncover patterns, trends, correlations, and other valuable information that can be used to solve real-world problems, enhance business strategies, optimize processes, and drive innovation in various domains such as business, healthcare, finance, marketing, and more.
